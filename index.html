About Us

Mawadda Group Making A Difference.

The Mawadda Group comprises four companies based in various cities across Kenya, focusing on four key sectors: the importation of motor vehicles from Japan, property management, cleaning services, and interior design expertise. Our journey began in 2016 in Mombasa, where we initially operated as brokers for vehicles, properties, and interior furnishings. Since then, we have grown substantially, now employing a team of at least 20 individuals.

Mission

Empowering with innovation, driving growth in diverse Kenyan industries.

Vision

Leading progress, fostering growth, and opportunity nationwide.
